WHAT'S TRACKED FOR EACH TRADE
================================================================================

Entry Information:
  • Symbol (TCS, INFY, HDFCBANK, etc.)
  • Signal type (BUY/SELL)
  • Entry price (real market price at signal time)
  • Entry time (timestamp with timezone)
  • Confidence level (0-100%)
  • Quantity (typically 1 lot)

Targets & Stops:
  • Projected Exit (calculated +2% for BUY, -2% for SELL)
  • Stop Loss (calculated -2% for BUY, +2% for SELL)
  • Target percentage

Results:
  • Actual Exit Price (closing price at market end)
  • Actual P&L (% gain/loss)
  • Trade Status (OPEN/CLOSED/HIT_TARGET/HIT_SL)
  • Hit actual target or stop loss?

CUSTOMIZATION
================================================================================

Change Profit Target:
  Edit paper_trader.py, line ~60:
  projected_exit = entry_price * 1.03  # Change 1.02 to 1.03 for 3% target

Change Stop Loss:
  Edit paper_trader.py, line ~61:
  stop_loss = entry_price * 0.97  # Change 0.98 to 0.97 for 3% SL

Add More Symbols:
